About-us

The Department of Computer Applications was established in the year 2006. The department offers three years course Bachelor of Computer Applications which aims at giving quality education to the students aspiring them to make careers in IT industry. In the year 2015, the department took an initiative to start a State Level IT Event "Technotronics" for BCA/BSc. (Computer Science) students from all over Goa. The students of eight different colleges from different parts of India participated in the event. This year "Technotronics" is being organized as National Level IT Event, we have invited various colleges from India and we have received positive response. Technotronics 2020 is a National Level IT Event this year . We are providing this platform for graduation level students studying computer science and applications on 3rd and 4th of March 2020 to showcase their technological talents and skills.

Rules

  • Each team representing their college must be officially deputed through the proper channel.
  • Each team comprises of 11+1 members that are bonafide students of the college, Registered for the BCA/BE/BSc. (Comp. Science) programs.
  • Only registered members of the team will be allowed to participate in the main event.
  • Once a team has been registered no change will be permitted in its composition thereafter. In genuine cases a substitution may be permitted only with the permission of the organizers..
  • The special Pre-event activity called Groupfie is organized with the primary aim to popularize the Technotronics National level IT event. Each team should post a pic on Instagram and will fetch 50 points for the team having maximum likes. (For more details refer Groupfie rules)
  • Participation in individual events will fetch the team 10 points.
  • The competitions are categorized into 3 categories i.e Gold, silver and bronze.

    Points allocated to the gold events are
    1st place: 50 points
    2nd place: 30 points

    Points allocated to the silver events are
    1st place: 40 points
    2nd place: 25 points

    Points allocated to the bronze events are
    1st place: 30 points
    2nd place: 20 points

  • Each team has to Report to the event venue on time as per the schedule.
  • Each registering team has to participate in at least 50% of the events organized.
  • Every member will have to wear identity cards that are provided throughout the event.
  • Each team has to preferably carry their own laptop.
  • The organizers reserve the right to cancel/modify the events/rules as seem to be appropriate.
  • The decisions of the organizers will be final and binding.
  • Any misbehavior at the event venue will lead to 50 point deduction.
  • Participant who is participating in gaming should not participate in any other event
  • Accommodation will be provided for "OUT STATION TEAMS".
